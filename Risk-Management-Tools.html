Risk Management Tools

Aviation operational risk assessment and safety management tools
✈️

FRAT

Flight Risk Assessment Tool used to evaluate operational flight risks, environmental conditions, crew factors, and mission complexity.

🧠

HFACS

Human Factors Analysis and Classification System used to identify, classify, and analyze human factors contributing to accidents, incidents, and operational events.

📊

Risk Overview

Review organizational risk trends, safety indicators, event distribution, and operational risk exposure.

🛠️

MEDA

Maintenance Error Decision Aid used to investigate maintenance-related events, human factors, contributing conditions, and prevention strategies.

🔎

REDA

Ramp Error Decision Aid used to investigate ramp handling events, identify contributing factors, human performance issues, and develop effective corrective actions.
