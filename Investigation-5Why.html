📌 1. What happened?

🔎 3. Why?

Go deeper into the condition, process, or human factor.

➜

⚠️ 5. Why?

Identify the system weakness or final contributing factor.

✅ Root Cause / Main Finding

This should represent the deepest process, control, training, supervision, or system weakness identified.
